The Complex

The Al-Fakhir Residential Towers are one of the most prominent luxury residential complexes in Baghdad, and are considered a modern architectural masterpiece. This project is being developed by “Al-Mars Company”, one of the companies of the Qaiwan Group, and is being built to the highest international quality standards.

The complex is located in Al-Qadisiyah District, near Karam Baghdadi Restaurant, and consists of three skyscraper towers with heights reaching 54 floors, 49 floors, and 46 floors, all overlooking the Tigris River. This exceptional project is close to Baghdad International Airport, as well as other strategic areas such as Al-Mansour and Al-Karada, placing it at the heart of the action.

The project represents a modern development that reflects the beauty and progress of the city of Baghdad, and also reflects the civilized face of the capital. The project focuses on environmental protection and reducing natural risks, in addition to providing all the necessary services to ensure the comfort and well-being of its residents.

The complex includes various facilities such as a shopping center, market, cafes, restaurants, parks, and children's playgrounds, in addition to green spaces carefully designed to provide an ideal living environment.
